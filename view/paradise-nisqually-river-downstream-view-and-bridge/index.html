Looking down the Nisqually River valley

You can see the path of Nisqually River from just below the glacier down to where it cuts through the forest.

This valley was originally carved by an enormous ancient glacier. There is still a glacier above here, but it's much smaller. Today, the river runs through the bottom of the valley.
