Log bridge

This is a log bridge for hikers to cross the river. There is a hand rail to make it easier to walk on. Even though it is made out of a tree trunk, it is long enough that it bounces when you walk over it.

This picture was taken on a hot day, so the river had more water flowing because of melting glacier ice. There is some water splashing onto the bridge!
